2. Platform Role (Very Important)

JHE HAUL is a technology marketplace that connects Customers with independent junk removal service providers ("Haulers").

The Company:

All junk removal services are performed by independent haulers, not by JHE HAUL.

The service contract exists solely between the Customer and the Hauler.

3. Property Removal Authorization

By posting a job and scheduling service, the Customer represents and warrants:

  1. They are the legal owner of the property to be removed OR

The Customer grants permission for the hauler to remove, haul away, and dispose of all items identified in the job listing, photographs, or present at the service location and designated for removal.

Once removed, items may be:

  • Disposed

  • Recycled
  • Resold
  • Destroyed

Removed items cannot be returned.
