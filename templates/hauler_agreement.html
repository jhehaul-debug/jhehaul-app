JHE HAUL PLATFORM HAULER AGREEMENT

Independent Contractor, Marketplace Participation & Non-Circumvention Agreement

Company: JHE HAUL, operated by J Hunter Enterprises Inc. ("Company")
Platform: JHE HAUL mobile and web application ("Platform")
User: Independent junk removal service provider ("Hauler")
Effective Date: Upon account registration

1. LEGAL ACCEPTANCE

By creating an account, logging in, bidding on a job, or accepting a job through the Platform, the Hauler agrees to this Agreement.

These actions constitute a legally binding electronic signature under the Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act (15 U.S.C. ยง7001).

If the Hauler does not agree, they must not use the Platform.

2. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

The Hauler is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, partner, or representative of the Company.

The Company:

  • Does not hire the Hauler
  • Does not supervise work
  • Does not control job performance
  • Does not provide equipment or labor
  • Does not guarantee jobs

The Hauler alone is responsible for:

  • Licenses
  • Insurance
  • Taxes
  • Workers
  • Vehicles
  • Disposal compliance
  • All applicable laws

3. PLATFORM ROLE

JHE HAUL is a technology marketplace only that connects customers with independent haulers.

The Company is NOT:

  • a junk removal company
  • a moving company
  • a contractor
  • a subcontractor
  • a carrier

The service contract exists solely between the Customer and the Hauler.

4. IN-APP COMMUNICATION REQUIREMENT

All communication must occur inside the Platform.

The Hauler shall not:

  • Exchange phone numbers
  • Exchange emails
  • Leave business cards
  • Provide outside contact information
  • Direct the customer off the app

5. NON-CIRCUMVENTION

The Hauler may not bypass the Platform.

The Hauler shall not perform services outside the Platform for any customer introduced through JHE HAUL.

This applies:

  • During use of the Platform
  • For two (2) years after leaving the Platform

Includes:

  • Repeat customers
  • Property managers
  • Referrals
  • Associated persons

Cancelling a job in the app and completing it privately is circumvention.

6. LIQUIDATED DAMAGES

Violation of Section 5 results in:

$2,500 per violation OR three (3) times the job value, whichever is greater.

The Company may also:

  • Remove the Hauler
  • Restrict access
  • Seek legal enforcement

7. BOOKING FEE, BID ACCEPTANCE & CUSTOMER PAYMENT

Hauler Bid

The Hauler submits a price ("Bid") for a job through the Platform. The Bid represents the total service price the Hauler is willing to perform the job for.

The Company does not set or control the Hauler's price.

Customer Booking Fee (Platform Fee)

When a customer accepts a Hauler's Bid, the customer must pay a required booking/platform fee to the Company in order to reserve the job.

The booking fee:

  • Is paid directly to JHE HAUL
  • Is not payment for junk removal services
  • Is a technology access, scheduling, and lead-generation fee
  • Is deducted from the Hauler's total Bid price

The Hauler has no control over the platform fee amount.

The booking fee is generally non-refundable except if:

  • The Hauler cancels the job, OR
  • The Company cannot provide a hauler for the scheduled service

Remaining Balance Paid to Hauler

After the job is completed, the customer pays the remaining balance of the Hauler's Bid directly to the Hauler.

The Company:

  • Does not collect the hauling payment
  • Does not hold hauling funds
  • Does not distribute hauling funds
  • Is not an escrow agent

The Hauler is solely responsible for:

  • Collecting the remaining payment
  • Refunds
  • Disputes
  • Receipts
  • Taxes

No Payment Obligation Owed by Hauler to Company

The Hauler does not pay the platform fee and never owes payment to the Company for a completed job.

The platform fee is paid solely by the customer as a booking and lead reservation fee associated with the Hauler's accepted Bid.

8. ACCOUNT TERMINATION

The Company may suspend or terminate the Hauler for:

  • Complaints
  • Fraud
  • Safety issues
  • Harassment
  • Off-platform contact
  • Circumvention

9. LIABILITY DISCLAIMER

JHE HAUL is not responsible for:

  • Property damage
  • Injury
  • Disposal legality
  • Hauler conduct
  • Worker actions
  • Job performance

All risks belong solely to the Hauler performing services.

10. INDEMNIFICATION

The Hauler agrees to indemnify and hold harmless JHE HAUL and J Hunter Enterprises Inc. from any claims, damages, lawsuits, or liabilities arising from the Hauler's services.

11. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Minnesota. All disputes must be brought in Minnesota courts.

12. DIGITAL SIGNATURE

By clicking "Create Account" or "I Agree", the Hauler provides a legally enforceable electronic signature equivalent to a handwritten signature.

13. MODIFICATIONS

The Company may update this Agreement at any time. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance.
