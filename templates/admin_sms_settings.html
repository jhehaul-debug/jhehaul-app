📋 Twilio Setup Instructions

Step 1: Create a Twilio account
Go to twilio.com → sign up (free trial includes test credits).

Step 2: Get your credentials
From the Twilio Console dashboard, copy your Account SID and Auth Token.

Step 3: Get a phone number
Console → Phone Numbers → Manage → Buy a number → choose an SMS-capable US number (~$1/month).

Step 4: Add environment variables
In DigitalOcean → App Platform → your app → Settings → Environment Variables, add:
TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID = your Account SID
TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN = your Auth Token
TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER = your Twilio number (e.g. +16125551234)
Optional: ADMIN_PHONE = your cell phone for admin SMS alerts

Step 5: Test
Use the "Send Test SMS" form above. Check SMS Logs to confirm delivery (look for a Twilio SID).

Trial account limits: Twilio trial accounts can only send to verified phone numbers. Upgrade to a paid account ($15–20/mo typical) to send to any US number.
