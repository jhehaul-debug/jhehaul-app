Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about local junk removal with JHE Haul.

How does junk removal work on JHE Haul?

Post a job with photos, local haulers send you price bids, you accept the best offer, pay a small deposit, and your hauler shows up and removes everything.

Can I get same-day junk pickup?

Yes — many local haulers on JHE Haul offer same-day or next-day pickup. When posting your job, just specify your preferred date and time.

How much does local junk removal cost?

Haulers set their own prices and bid on your job, so costs depend on what you need removed and your location. Because multiple haulers compete, you typically pay less than calling a single company.

Is my address safe?

Yes. Haulers only see your ZIP code until you accept a bid and pay the deposit. Your full pickup address is only shared after payment is confirmed.

What can local haulers remove?

Furniture, appliances, garage junk, yard debris, construction debris, office equipment, electronics, hot tubs, storage unit contents, and most bulk household items. Post photos and describe your load — haulers will bid on it.

How do I become a hauler?

Sign up with Google, choose the hauler role, set your home ZIP and travel radius, and start bidding on jobs near you immediately. No subscription fees — you only earn when you complete jobs.
